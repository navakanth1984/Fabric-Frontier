## Key Idea
Regularly documenting and reviewing processes for issue resolution helps to identify areas of inefficiency and implement improvements.

## Details
- Documenting processes involves creating a clear record of each step taken during the resolution process, including any decisions made or actions taken.
- Reviewing these processes regularly allows teams to identify patterns or inconsistencies that may be contributing to issues, and make targeted improvements.
- Regular review can also help to reduce the time it takes to resolve issues, as well as improve overall quality.
