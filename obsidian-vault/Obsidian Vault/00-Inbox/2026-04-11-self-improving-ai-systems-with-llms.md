## Key Idea
The development of AI agents that can loop and improve themselves through the use of Large Language Models (LLMs) is a rapidly advancing field.

## Details
* These systems leverage the power of LLMs to generate new text, identify patterns, and optimize their own performance.
* The self-improvement aspect allows these agents to adapt and evolve in response to changing environments and data distributions.
